The Best Movies According to Farhan

My top 3 movies of all-times

All Quiet in Western Front

An excellent movies for me, its captured the situation when at war, particularly in 1914 as German Forces advancing into French Territories.

Peninsula

A Korean zombie film, based on South Korea after the prequel movie named Train to Busan. Here after 2 years, South Korea goverment has been destroyed by the horde of zombies, all the remaining citizen either fleet via ships to neighbouring countries or to the North, where North Korea lies.

Kingdom

Actually its a series, setting In Joseon Dynasty where the Joseon of Josen Empire is resurrected using resurrection herb, its succesfully made the Emperor lives again, but with different personality.
